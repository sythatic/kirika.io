Primordial Energy


Primordial Energy is the basis of all other scientific and magical practivces in the series. With theoretically limitless power, it has the ability to control all aspects of the universe. Based upon energy manipulation, It follows the idea that energy is everywhere in the universe and that the energy can be controlled.

Arcturan Law of Energy

In terms of potential power, disciplines based on the Arcturan Law of Energy helm all others. Many ancient races, most notably the Arcturan Celestials, believed that mastery of primordial energy could unlock the ability to contol the universe in its entierty.
