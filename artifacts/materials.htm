Primordial Energy is the most powerful from of magic in the Tales of Seven universe. It is the basis of all other forms of magic in the series. With theoretically limitless power, it has the ability to control all aspects of the universe. Based upon energy manipulation, It follows the idea that energy is everywhere in the universe and that the energy can be controlled.
