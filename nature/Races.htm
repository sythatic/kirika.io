    Virtually featureless, vaguely-humanoid being made entirely out of a cellular protoplasmic mass, which allows it to split different parts of its body off to create other semi-independent entities when killed, they will disintegrate into Protoplasm. It is spawned from the Matter Codex.

    Amorphous. The jelly can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch wide without squeezing.

    Freedom of Movement. Ignores difficult terrain and can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.

    Invincibility. When transparent, it is invincible, it cannot perform any melee attacks in this form.

    Unusual Nature. Does not need to eat, drink, sleep, or breathe.

    Magma. The Elemental Plasm will turn molten and surround the floor with fire.

    Actions

    Pseudopod. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (2d6 + 2) bludgeoning damage plus 3 (1d6) acid damage.

    Lattice.The Elemental Plasm will turn into a glass cube that slowly tumbles around the stage.

    Reactions

    Split. When the Plasm Wraith takes 10 or more damage from a single attack or instance of a damaging effect, it can use its reaction to split plasm off of itself. This causes the Plasm Wraith to decrease its size by one category. For every size category below Huge the Plasm Wraith is, its speed increases by 5 feet. The Plasm Wraith cannot use this reaction if its size is currently Tiny. The plasm uses the statistics of anochre jelly except it is an aberration instead of an ooze and is the same size as the Plasm Wraith's new size. The plasm has hit points equal to the damage taken (up to a maximum of 45). The plasmtakes its turn immediately after the Plasm Wraith and acts as its ally.

    Reabsorb. If a plasm ends its turn within 5 feet of the Plasm Wraith, the Plasm Wraith can use its reaction to reabsorb the plasm. The plasm dies and the Plasm Wraith regains hit points equal to the number of hit points the Plasm Wraith had. If the plasm was the same size as the plasmwraith or larger, the Plasm Wraith's size category increases by 1 when it uses this reaction.

    Mythic Actions

    If the Plasm Wraith'sFrenzytrait is active, it can use the Mythic Action options below as legendary actions.

    Needle Arm.This mythic action has different properties based on the Plasm Wraith's size.

    If the Plasm Wraith uses this Mythic Action whileHuge or larger, it makes a single melee attack with +11 to hit and a 20 foot reach. The attack deals 26 (6d6 + 5) piercing damage on a hit.

    If the Plasm Wraith uses this Mythic Action while Large, it makes two melee attacks, each with +8 to hit and a 15 foot reach. Each attack deals 16(4d6 + 2) piercing damage on a hit.

    If the Plasm Wraith uses this Mythic Actionwhile Medium, it makes three melee attacks, each with +6 to hit and a 10 foot reach. Each attack deals 7 (2d6) piercing damage on a hit.

    If the Plasm Wraith uses this Mythic Actionwhile Small, it makes three melee attacks, each with +6 to hit and a 10 foot reach. Each attack deals 5(2d4) piercing damage on a hit.

    If the Plasm Wraith uses this Mythic Actionwhile Tiny, it makes fourmelee attacks with disadvantage, each with +6 to hit and a 5foot reach. Each attack deals 2(1d4) piercing damage on a hit.

    Spell (Costs 2 Actions).The Plasm Wraith casts a spell using its Innate Spellcasting trait.
